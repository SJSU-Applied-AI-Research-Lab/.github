# Applied AI Research Lab

The Applied AI Research Lab at San José State University develops reproducible and impactful research in applied artificial intelligence.

## Research Areas

- Agentic AI and multi-agent systems
- Healthcare AI and clinical language models
- Cybersecurity AI
- Multimodal learning
- Trustworthy machine learning
- MLOps and LLMOps

## Featured Projects

- PE2Prompt
- VisioRAG
- TinyPulse+
- AgentSecBench
- Clinical Agentic AI
